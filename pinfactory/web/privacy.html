This Privacy Policy explains how the pinfactory application ("the app", operated by [YOUR NAME OR BUSINESS]) handles information when it is used to publish content to Pinterest on behalf of its operator.

Who this app is for

pinfactory is a self-hosted tool that its operator runs to create and schedule Pinterest Pins for their own books and Pinterest account. It does not offer accounts to, or act on behalf of, other users.

Information the app uses

  • Your Pinterest account access. When you authorize the app, Pinterest provides an access token and refresh token so the app can create boards and Pins on your account via the Pinterest API. These tokens are stored locally by the operator (and, if the operator runs it on a scheduler, in that service's encrypted secrets store). They are never shared with third parties.
  • Your catalogue data. Book titles, descriptions, links, cover images, and related metadata that the operator supplies, stored locally to generate Pins and their captions.
  • Pinterest analytics. If enabled, the app may read impression, save, and click metrics for Pins it created, to produce private performance reports.

How the information is used

  • To create boards and publish Pins to the operator's own Pinterest account.
  • To generate Pin titles and descriptions (optionally using the Anthropic API, which receives only the book metadata needed to write the caption).
  • To produce private analytics summaries for the operator.

The app does not sell data, does not use it for advertising to third parties, and does not collect information about anyone other than its operator's own account and catalogue.

Storage & retention

Data is stored locally in a database on the operator's own machine or hosting environment for as long as the operator keeps it. Pinterest access and refresh tokens can be revoked at any time from your Pinterest account settings, which immediately ends the app's access.

Third-party services

  • Pinterest API — to publish Pins and read their analytics (Pinterest Privacy Policy).
  • Anthropic API (optional) — to draft Pin captions from book metadata (Anthropic Privacy Policy).

Your choices

You can revoke the app's Pinterest access at any time in your Pinterest settings, and delete the app's local data at any time.
