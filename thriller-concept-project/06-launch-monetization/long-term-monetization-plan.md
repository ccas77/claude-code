# Long-Term Monetization Plan

How *FEED* compounds from a first book into a durable indie business over 3–4 years. All figures flagged **[est.]** are industry aggregates (see `../research-data/economics.md`), not guarantees. First-year income is usually small and often net-negative; this is a **backlist-building investment** whose payoff arrives with scale.

---

## The thesis: monetize one acquisition many times

The unit of profit is the **series**, not the book. Acquisition (ads/promos/BookTok) is paid once at Book 1; revenue compounds through **read-through × backlist depth × format stacking × channel layering.** Every decision below serves that compounding.

---

## Revenue layer cake (stacked over time)

```
YEAR 3+  ▐ Special editions · foreign rights · film/TV option · audio full series · direct storefront
YEAR 2   ▐ Box sets · audio (Bk1→) · direct backlist · deeper KU pages · Books 4–6
YEAR 1   ▐ Ebook sales + KU page-reads (Books 1–3) · print · newsletter list-building
         └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
           acquisition paid once ──► read-through compounds across everything above
```

---

## Stage 1 — Launch & prove (Months 0–12)
- **Rapid-release Books 1–3**, KU-exclusive, $2.99→$4.99 ladder; print for trust.
- Build the **reader magnet → newsletter → ARC** funnel (the low-cash acquisition system).
- **Primary revenue:** ebook + KU page-reads. **Target:** prove **Book 1→2 read-through ≥50%** (the health line). *(economics.md §3)*
- **Reality:** likely low or net-negative after covers/editing/ads — expected and fine. *(economics.md §9)*

## Stage 2 — Compound (Months 12–30)
- **Books 4–6** + **Box Set (1–3)** at $9.99; the series crosses the **Book 3–5 "breakout" line** where read-through and also-boughts compound. *(economics.md §3)*
- **Start audiobook** on Book 1 (proven LTV) → new channel, over-performs on this voice-driven concept.
- **Start direct sales** of backlist/box sets via Payhip + BookFunnel (~95% margin).
- **Newsletter** now a real asset → cheaper, warmer launches; ARC team scales.

## Stage 3 — Scale & diversify (Year 3+)
- **Backlist compounding:** deep catalogs (25+ books) reach **median ~$3,000/mo [est.]**; the connected-standalone engine lets *FEED* run long without a co-writer. *(economics.md §9)*
- **Full-series audio**, **special/collector editions** (direct, high-margin for superfans), **translations/foreign rights**, and — given the high-concept, screen-native hook — a credible **film/TV option** path (comps: McFadden, Dawson, Ross all optioned). Options are found money, not a plan.
- **Test wide** (Apple/Kobo/Google) selectively once direct + audience reduce KU dependence.

---

## The monetization mechanics that matter most (ranked)
1. **Read-through** — the master lever. Protect it with cover/blurb/Book-1-ending discipline and rapid release.
2. **Backlist depth** — more books = more entry points, more also-boughts, more KU pages. The series engine exists to make depth cheap.
3. **KU page-reads** — passive, stacking, binge-rewarded income on a high-completion concept.
4. **Newsletter/list** — the owned asset that de-risks every future launch and enables direct.
5. **Format stacking (audio/print/direct)** — each proven title re-monetized 3–4×.
6. **Rights/options** — asymmetric upside; don't count on it, be ready for it.

---

## Realistic trajectory (illustrative, not a promise) [est.]
| Year | State | Income posture |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Books 1–3, funnel built | Small / net-negative — investment phase |
| 2 | Books 4–6, box set, audio Bk1, direct backlist | Approaching "active author" range (~$12–15k/yr territory) |
| 3–4 | 8–12+ books, full audio, direct store, options | Path to full-time as backlist + read-through + formats compound |

**Bottom line:** *FEED* is built to be a **compounding backlist business**, not a lottery ticket. The concept's single-craft buildability keeps production fast, the connected-standalone engine keeps the backlist growing on author brand, and the channel roadmap layers format and margin only after lifetime value is proven — the disciplined path indie thriller economics actually reward.
