# Indie Thriller Concept — Complete Market-to-Manuscript Package

**Prepared:** July 2026
**Purpose:** Build a market-ready thriller book/series concept for a solo indie author, starting from open-internet research — choosing the thriller subgenre by evidence, then designing the strongest defensible, original, commercially promising concept.

**How this package was built:** A multi-agent research-and-design pipeline. Parallel research agents evaluated 15 thriller subgenres for indie-author commercial potential; a synthesis agent scored them; skeptic agents adversarially attacked the winning choice; a concept tournament generated competing concepts scored by a judge panel; skeptics stress-tested the winner; a completeness critic verified every deliverable against the Definition of Done.

## Core constraints this concept was designed around
- **Solo indie economics:** no advance, no institutional access, no celebrity platform, modest budget, AI-assisted drafting.
- **Series-first revenue:** read-through across a series is the primary monetization engine.
- **Kindle Unlimited native:** designed for the page-read (KENP) economy plus ebook/paperback/audio.
- **Fast path to first test:** realistic production scope for one author.
- **Evidence over preference:** the subgenre and concept were selected by research, not taste.

> Sourcing note: Market figures are drawn from public sources (retailer category data, industry genre reports, self-publishing surveys, reader communities) and are cited inline. Where a number is an estimate or from a secondary source, it is flagged as such. Nothing in this package was published, uploaded, or submitted anywhere — all deliverables are local.
